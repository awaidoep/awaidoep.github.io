Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about overseas employment

How long does the process take?

The complete process typically takes 3-6 months from application to deployment, depending on the country and job type.

What are the costs involved?

We charge a transparent service fee that's a percentage of your first month's salary. There are no hidden charges.

Do you guarantee job placement?

We match qualified candidates with verified employers. Placement depends on your skills, experience, and employer requirements.

Which documents do I need?

You'll need a valid passport, educational certificates, experience letters, medical fitness certificate, and police clearance.

Can I choose my preferred country?

Yes, you can specify preferred countries. We'll match you with opportunities in those locations based on your profile.

What support do you provide after deployment?

We provide post-arrival support including airport pickup, accommodation assistance, workplace orientation, and emergency help.
